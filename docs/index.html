Features

Built for privacy-first workflows

Every feature designed to keep your voice data on your machine and your transcription fast.

Fully Local

Your audio never leaves your Mac. No cloud APIs, no accounts, no data collection. Powered by whisper.cpp running natively on Apple Silicon.

Metal Accelerated

Hardware-accelerated inference using Apple's Metal GPU framework. The tiny model transcribes in under a second on M-series chips.

Configurable Hotkey

Double-tap Right Option by default. Customize the trigger key and timing window to match your workflow.

Cursor Injection

Transcribed text is pasted directly at your cursor position in any application. Clipboard contents are preserved automatically.

Three Profiles

Switch between Fast, Balanced, and Quality modes. Each profile uses a different Whisper model sized for the task at hand.

Menubar Native

Lives quietly in your menubar with no dock icon. Visual and audio feedback for recording state. Designed to stay out of your way.

Setup

Up and running in two minutes

No accounts. No API keys. Just build and go.

01

Clone & build

Clone the repo, run the whisper.cpp build script, then swift build. Or use the bundle script to get a proper .app.

02

Download a model

Open Aavaz from your menubar and click "Download Current Model." The tiny.en model is just 75 MB and downloads in seconds.

03

Start talking

Double-tap Right Option to start recording. Tap again to stop. Your transcription appears at the cursor instantly.
